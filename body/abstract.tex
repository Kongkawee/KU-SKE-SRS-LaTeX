Spell Splash is a turn-based RPG educational game designed to enhance English vocabulary retention through interactive gameplay. The game integrates multiple AI components to provide an engaging and adaptive learning experience. Aimed at individuals who struggle with traditional learning methods, Spell Splash offers an alternative approach that combines entertainment with education. By utilizing AI-driven mechanics, the game personalizes challenges, tracks progress, and ensures an effective learning process tailored to each playerâ€™s needs. The project seeks to make vocabulary acquisition more accessible, enjoyable, and efficient for learners of all levels.
